Learn about the Fennec fox



Scientific Name: Vulpes zerda

Diet: Omnivore

WEIGHT: 2.2 to 3.3 pounds

Habitat: Desert

A.K.A the desert fox. The fennec fox is a small fox found in desert zones of North Africa as well as the Sinai and Arabian peninsulas. They are nocturnal and avoid the heat of daytime. Their huge ear help them regulate body heat and keep them cool. Their fur also helps keep them warm during cold nights and protection them from the hot sun. They can jump up tp 2 feet in the air and leap a distance of 4 feet. They hang in groups of up to ten foxes and like dogs will pee to mark their territory. Their diet contains plants , rodents, eggs, reptiles, and insects.
