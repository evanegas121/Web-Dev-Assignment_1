Come say hi to our penguins!



Scientific Name: Aptenodytes forsteri

Diet: Carnivore

Size: 45 inches

Weight: up to 88 lbs

Habitat: Antartica

The Emperor penguin will be found on the Antartic ice or waters. Penguins will breed in the winter after several weeks of courtship. A female penguin lays one egg which the father will balance on his feet and cover with his pouch to keep it warm. The mothers will go away to sea and return after two months, bringing food that they will regurgitate to feed the newborn chicks. Young penguins will often be in huddles while the parents are away.
